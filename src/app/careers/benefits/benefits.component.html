Company Benefits

Online Material Sourcing seeks to become the major player in the material sourcing industry by offering above market pay and benefits in order to attract top talent.

Work From Home

As an online platform we will leverage our immediate lack of need for a corporate headquarters by offering the ability to work from home to all employees.

Paid Time Off

All employees will have the ability to take up to 15 days per year off, not including any holidays or bereavement. This time combines the traditional vacation and sick leave into the simpler Paid Time Off and accrues by a set amount as the year progresses. In other words, not all of your total earnable leave is available on day 1.

Insurance
