Our Mission

The mission of OMS is to improve the efficiency of the buying and selling process within the industrial materials world. We will push the industry forward with a relentless drive toward automation of repetitive, inefficient processes and the adopting of the newest technologies allowing us to pivot as needed.

Our Culture, Our Drive

Helmed by four experienced and motivated individuals, OMS features both a loose atmosphere and an intense air of “go, Go, GO!”. Aiming to change an industry that can be laughably stuck in it’s ways, the members of OMS know full well their task will not be an easy one.

Having experienced firsthand the tediousness that is taking a large RFQ and entering into one’s own system, the OMS team know that the current process leaves much to be desired. Recognizing that a properly configured, automated system can handle this process near instantaneously, and that the actual process as performed by sellers in response to potential buyers, is not a value add. The faster they can get such requests entered into their systems, the faster they can respond and the greater the likelihood of quote acceptance.
